# BDA Adult Dyslexia Checklist
Source: British Dyslexia Association

## Questions (Rarely / Occasionally / Often / Most of the time)
1. Do you confuse visually similar words such as cat and cot? (scores: 3/6/9/12)
2. Do you lose your place or miss out lines when reading? (scores: 2/4/6/8)
3. Do you confuse the names of objects, for example table for chair? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
4. Do you have trouble telling left from right? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
5. Is map reading or finding your way to a strange place confusing? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
6. Do you re-read paragraphs to understand them? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
7. Do you get confused when given several instructions at once? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
8. Do you make mistakes when taking down telephone messages? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
9. Do you find it difficult to find the right word to say? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
10. How often do you think of creative solutions to problems? (scores: 1/2/3/4)

## Questions (Easy / Challenging / Difficult / Very Difficult)
11. How easy do you find it to sound out words such as e-le-phant? (scores: 3/6/9/12)
12. When writing, do you find it difficult to organise your thoughts on paper? (scores: 2/4/6/8)
13. Did you learn your multiplication tables easily? (scores: 2/4/6/8)
14. How easy do you find it to recite the alphabet? (scores: 1/2/3/4)
15. How hard do you find it to read aloud? (scores: 1/2/3/4)

## Scoring
- Total 45+ = likely dyslexic indicators
- Total 30-44 = some indicators
- Total below 30 = few indicators
